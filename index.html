Doll Care & Style Guide

🧸 Doll Care Basics

Keep Your Doll Looking Beautiful

  • Brush hair gently — Use a wire wig brush, starting from the ends
  • Avoid direct sunlight — Prevents fading and material damage
  • Store properly — Keep in a cool, dry place away from moisture
  • Handle with clean hands — Oils and dirt can stain over time
  • Rotate outfits — Prevents fabric stress and color transfer
Pro Tip: For tangled hair, use a small amount of fabric softener mixed with water as a detangling spray.

When to Seek Professional Help

  • Loose limbs or broken joints
  • Deep stains that won't come out
  • Hair that's severely matted
  • Missing or damaged eyes

👚 Outfit Ideas

Endless Style Combinations

  • Layer pieces — Cardigans over dresses, vests over shirts
  • Mix patterns — Stripes with florals (keep colors complementary)
  • Accessorize — Hats, scarves, and jewelry transform any outfit
  • Seasonal themes — Pastels for spring, cozy knits for winter
  • Occasion dressing — Casual play clothes vs. special event outfits
Style Secret: Start with one statement piece (like a handmade dress) and build around it with simple accessories.

Must-Have Basics

  • 2-3 versatile dresses
  • Mix-and-match tops and bottoms
  • One special occasion outfit
  • Cozy pajamas and robe
  • Seasonal outerwear

📦 Smart Storage

Organize Without the Clutter

  • Shoe organizers — Over-the-door pockets are perfect for doll clothes
  • Small bins — Separate by category (tops, bottoms, dresses, accessories)
  • Drawer dividers — Keep small items like shoes and jewelry sorted
  • Hanging organizers — Use doll-sized hangers for dresses and coats
  • Clear containers — See everything at a glance
Space Saver: Store off-season clothes in labeled bins and rotate them quarterly.

DIY Storage Solutions

  • Shoe boxes covered in pretty paper
  • Small baskets from dollar stores
  • Repurpose mint tins for tiny accessories
  • Hang a small curtain rod for doll hangers

✨ Gentle Cleaning

Safe Cleaning Methods

  • Spot clean first — Try a damp cloth before full washing
  • Use mild soap — Baby shampoo or gentle detergent only
  • Cold water — Hot water can shrink or fade fabrics
  • Air dry flat — Never use a dryer; reshape while damp
  • Test first — Check for colorfastness on a hidden area
Stain Removal: For tough stains, make a paste of baking soda and water, apply gently, let sit 15 minutes, then rinse.

What NOT to Do

  • Don't use bleach or harsh chemicals
  • Don't wring or twist delicate fabrics
  • Don't dry in direct sunlight
  • Don't iron without a pressing cloth

📏 Size Guide

Understanding Doll Sizes

  • 18 inch dolls — American Girl, Our Generation, My Life As
  • 15 inch dolls — Bitty Baby, some baby dolls
  • 8 inch dolls — Mini dolls, some collectible dolls
Size Check: Measure your doll from head to toe. Also measure chest, waist, and hips for best fit.

Will It Fit?

  • Check the seller's size chart
  • Read reviews for fit feedback
  • When in doubt, size up — easier to take in than let out
  • Stretchy fabrics are more forgiving
